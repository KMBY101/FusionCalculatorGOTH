  • Pick two Personae you carry (your Starter Persona can never be used).
  • Cross-reference their Arcana on the Fusion Reference Table to find the resulting Arcana.
  • The new Persona's level can be at most the average of the two source levels, rounded down — and never higher than your own Character Level.
  • Pick any Persona of that Arcana at or under the level cap.
  • You get 5 + PLv Spell Points (plus Confidant bonuses) to buy spells from the new Persona's own Types.
  • You separately get half the new Persona's level in SP (half-points count) to inherit spells from the two source Personae's Type Decks, ignoring Type restrictions — these points vanish if unspent.
  • If the Arcana rolled is Judgement, you may take an Aeon Persona instead, if your table uses the Sea of Souls add-on.
